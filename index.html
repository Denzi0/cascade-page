Who We Are

About Us

At Intracare Services, we specialise in disability specific (paediatric and adult) occupational therapy - functional capacity and housing assessments, daily living skills capacity building, assistive technology and accommodation transition planning.

Our AHPRA registered therapists work with managing various disabilities to help improve quality of life and independence.

The NDIS funds occupational therapy (OT) supports that are deemed reasonable and necessary, directly related to a participant's disability. These supports can include assistance with daily living activities, development of fine motor skills, and recommendations for assistive technology or home modifications. The specific supports funded depend on individual goals and assessed needs.
